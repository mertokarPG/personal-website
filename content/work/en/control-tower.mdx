## Problem

Ops teams alt-tabbed between 7 systems to answer one question: what is the line actually doing right now? Every outage turned into a forensic scavenger hunt across SAP, Proficy, recipe apps, and PLC screens.

## Approach

1. Workshops with operators, line leaders, and quality. Wrote down the 12 questions people asked every shift.
2. Drew the data model around those questions, not around the source systems. Back-end aggregates; UI shows, it doesn't compute.
3. Built it read-only on purpose. One less cybersecurity conversation, zero risk of mutating plant state.
4. iRisk assessment + technical/environment diagrams in parallel with build so app registration was ready when we shipped.
